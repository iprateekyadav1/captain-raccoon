# Captain Raccoon Brand Guidelines

## Theme
Cinematic night-harbor adventure with cozy storytelling vibes.

## Colors
- **Navy (Backgrounds)**: `#0B132B`
- **Teal (Secondary Backgrounds)**: `#1C3738`
- **Amber/Gold (Accents/Lights)**: `#FFAA00` / `#FFD166`
- **Off-white (Text)**: `#F8F9FA`
- **Muted Text**: `#9BA4B5`

## Typography
- **Headings (Display)**: `Outfit`, adventurous, bold, slightly rounded.
- **Body (Sans-serif)**: `Inter`, clean, comfortable line-height.

## Visual Style
- Mix of clean modern UI and illustrated world-building (ocean, pier, lights).
- Subtle gradients, soft glows, and blurred backgrounds.
- Gentle parallax and floating animations for small background shapes.
